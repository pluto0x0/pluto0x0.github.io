TD( λ\lambdaλ )

For n-step bootstrap, give a (1−λ)λn(1-\lambda)\lambda^n(1−λ)λn weight.

  • λ=0\lambda = 0λ=0 : Only n=1 gives the full weight. TD(0).
  • λ→1\lambda \to 1λ→1 : (almost) Monte-Carlo.
